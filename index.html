Projects

Here's a few of the 14 projects I did as a part of my course work!

C Graphics Project

My team of 3 designed a project to demonstrate the usage of graphics in C. We experimented with various mathematical functions to depict basic shapes. Our final submission included rain, a sunrise and a rainbow.

Java: Space Invaders

As our 5th semester project, my partner and I developed code for the popular game ‘Space Invaders’. We use javafx and showed the use of various concepts we learnt in our lectures such as Inheritance, Static & This keyword, access specifiers, Objects, Loops, Arrays etc.

HTML: Web Design

In this project, we showed the use of HTML, CSS and Javascript and designed an interactive website on the not so popular idea of ‘Book Cafes’. We used various design and dynamic HTML concept which enables the users to browse through a list of books and meals, add them to their cart, and make user accounts to pay for the same.

Eunoia- Final Project

As a part of our final project, my team and I created a student app using Flutter that allows the user to create a To-Do list, upload notes and have a virtual study ambiance with music

Research Paper

ABSTRACT:In recent years, there have been numerous improvements and changes in the field of STEM. Many researches showed that visual programming languages like Scratch and programming have a positive effect on understanding of concepts. In this paper, we aim to discover the relationship between coding practices and understanding of CS and mathematical concepts.

Descriptive and Applied Statistics Project

In this project, my team of 4 did a presentation describing in detail the change in agricultural practices over the past decade. We took data from official Indian statistics websites such as NSSO, NSO, NCRB, and applied concepts learnt to it. In order to make the presentation more appealing to other students at home, we used memes in order to explain our working.
